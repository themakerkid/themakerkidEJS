What is Technology?


Technology is the use of knowledge to invent new devices or tools. Throughout history, technology has made people's lives easier. Although we might think of technology as being the internet, mobile devices and computer game, in reality it has been with us since early the earliest humans. Early humans set themselves apart from other animals when they learned to control fire. Later examples of technology were tools and weapons, the printing press, the telephone and Robots

Steve Jobs

was an American information technology entrepreneur and inventor. He was co-founder of Apple Inc., maker of the Macintosh computer, the iPod, the iPhone and the iPad. He was CEO and majority shareholder of Pixar Animation Studios.

Tim Berners-Lee

is an English computer scientist, best known as the inventor of the World Wide Web. He wrote the first web browser and server in 1990, while employed at CERN in Switzerland. He is Director of the World Wide Web Consortium (W3C)

Martha Lane Fox

(born 10 February 1973) is a British businesswoman, philanthropist, and public servant. She founded co-founded Lastminute.com in the dotcom boom of the early 2000, and is a is a board member of Twitter, mydeco.com and Marks & Spencer,

Bill Gates

is an American business magnate, entrepreneur, philanthropist, investor, and programmer. In 1975, Gates and Paul Allen co-founded Microsoft, which became the world's largest PC software company.
