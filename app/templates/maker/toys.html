Cool Toys

Snap Circuits 4/5

Snap Circuits is a toy that allows you to create circuits but with snap together pieces. It is safer than normal circuits and you program in a language called BASIC.

Pros:

  • It is easier on the eye because they are big pieces.
  • BASIC is easier than Arduino C++
  • Kid-friendly

Cons:

  • It is quite expensive compared to buying regular electronics components.
  • It is limited in the amount of components.

Thanks to Home Science Tools for the video.


Leonardo Da Vinci Catapult 4/5

This toy is a catapult that can be adjusted. You pull the swing arm down and pull the string and it shoots! It would assemble everything without glue and then glue it all together (I did that to mine).

Pros:

  • It launches the object quite far.
  • It is fairly easy to make (you could start and finish building it on the same day).
  • It is fun to make.

Cons:

  • You could hurt your hand when the swing arm comes down.
  • Young kids might shoot an object into someone's eye.

Thanks to GreatKidsToys for the video.


Rubik's Cube 4/5

The Rubik's Cube is a logic puzzle that can only be solved if you know the solution (unless you break apart the cube and put it back together or peel off all the stickers and stick them back on). My record is 45 seconds using an Advanced method.

Pros:

  • The solution is fairly easy to learn (it requires some memorising).
  • It is amazing to see someone solve it fast.
  • It is entertaining.

Cons:

  • It can annoy some people after a while.
  • It can be frustrating to memorise all the algorithims.



River Crossing 4/5

River Crossing is another logic puzzle that can also be frustrating. You must move planks around to get to the other side (Level 40 is the hardest!).

Pros:

  • It exercises your brain.
  • It can be fun on a rainy day.

Cons:

  • It might seem like it is for babies (like my Mum said to me when we saw it in Easons).



Meccanoid 5/5

Meccanoid is a voice controlled robot that can tell jokes and dance. It has two models: Meccanoid G15 and Meccanoid G15KS. Meccanoid G15 is the model shown in the video. Meccanoid G15KS is the same as the Meccanoid G15 except that is has eight motors on the model and the Meccanoid G15 has four and it is red in colour.

Pros:

  • It can record movements and play them back (L.I.M).
  • It is fun to play with (as I said, it can tell jokes).
  • It can be controlled from your phone or tablet. (This time you can use Apple.)

Cons:

  • It is quite expensive.
