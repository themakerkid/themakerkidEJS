Limor Fried

Steve Jobs

was an American information technology entrepreneur and inventor. He was co-founder of Apple Inc., maker of the Macintosh computer, the iPod, the iPhone and the iPad. He was CEO and majority shareholder of Pixar Animation Studios.

Adam Savage

is an American industrial designer and special effects fabricator, actor, educator, and TV personality on the Discovery Channel TV series MythBusters. His model work has appeared in major films, including Star Wars: Episode II and The Matrix Reloaded.
