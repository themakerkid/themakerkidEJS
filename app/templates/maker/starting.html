Getting Started

  • So how do I get started?

    To get started making stuff with electronics, there are some basic tools and components that you will need. If you just want to experiment with some simple circuits, all you will need is a basic kit consisting of a breadboard, a battery, and some electronic components like resistors, capacitors, LEDs and diodes. Please go to the Where do I get this stuff? section below for more information. Now let's get started by learning about some of these simple components.

    Adafruit Circuit Playground.

  • So how do I connect all of it up?

    In order to make a circuit you will need some way of connecting all the components and wires together, For this you will need a breadboard. Also you will need some way of powering the circuit. For this you can use a battery. You can also power the circuit by using a power adapter, like a mobile phone charger with an appropriate adapter.

    Breadboards and Circuit Construction

    Solderless breadboards are a device designed to allow the quick construction of an electronic circuit. They are often used in circuit design and testing. This video demonstrates the basic use of a breadboard. You can make cool stuff just using some electronic components, but if you want to control your circuit using code, you will need a micro controller like an Arduino, or a small single board computer like the Raspberry Pi. Please go to the Arduino section to learn more.

    Video Courtesy of ScienceOnline. Thank you!

  • Where do I get this stuff?

    You can get this stuff on Amazon, CPC Ireland or on Radionics.


  • And what tools do I need?

    If you are just getting started building all your circuits on a breadboard, and you have some patch cables, then you don't need to get any tools just yet. If you are want to make your keep your circuit creations permanently, then you will need to get some basic tools. You will also need to learn how to solder. Your parents or guardians may have some of this stuff lying around the house already.
