Events & Competitions



Coder Dojo — Coolest Projects

Cooles Projects is a tech event for the world’s youngest innovators, creators and entrepreneurs!. It is run by the CoderDojo foundation. Although the showcase event is held in Ballsbridge, the awards are a Europe-wide event, with participants from the UK, Poland, Spain, Italy, Portugal, Belgium, Holland, Romania and Turkey. The Coolest Projects Awards was created to encourage, support and showcase the creativity within the CoderDojo community. With CoderDojo, young people can make their most exciting ideas a reality. Please click Coolest Projects. for more information.




Eir Junior Spiders

The awards recognise and reward youths aged 19 and under for the innovative ways they use the internet, especially around website development, school blogs and design. ntries are open to everyone aged between 4-19 years of age from the Republic or Northern Ireland. Those who enter are representing themselves and their School/Coderdojos/Education Centres. It’s great fun to design your own website or web app and it is also a very useful skill to have. Please click Eir Junior Spiders for more information.



Irish Scratch National Finals

The competition is open to students in primary and second level schools in Ireland, and to students attending after-school coding clubs such as Coderdojo. Projects can be about anything – the only limit is students’ imagination. Please click Scratch National Competition for more information.



All Ireland Programming Olympiad

This is an annual computer programming olympiad held at Dublin City University (DCU). It amins to encourage and reward young programmers, (male and female) and to promote contacts among programmers in Ireland. It is used to select a team for the International Olympiad in Informatics (IOI). The competition is open to all second-level students in Ireland who are under 20 on the 1st of July. Please click AIPO for more information.
