What is App Inventor?

App Inventor lets you develop applications for Android devices using a web browser and either a connected phone or an emulator. The App Inventor servers store your work and help you keep track of your projects. It is kind of like Scratch but for Android devices.

Getting Started

This is a video by MIT App Inventor showing you how to make a MagicTrick app. In this video you will make an app that makes a rabbit appear when you touch the hat or shake the device. For this video you will need a laptop and a phone or tablet.

  • Setup Instructions: appinventor.mit.edu/explore/ai2/setup.html
  • You can find the images and sound here: appinventor.mit.edu/explore/media-library.html
