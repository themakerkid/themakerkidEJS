Famous Coders

  • Ada Lovelace
  • Tim Berners-Lee
  • Guido van Rossum
  • Linus Torvalds

Ada Lovelace

A gifted mathematician, Ada is considered to have written instructions for the first computer program in the mid-1800s. She died in 1852. In 1980, the U.S. Department of Defense named a newly developed computer language "Ada," after her.

Tim Berners-Lee

is an English computer scientist, best known as the inventor of the World Wide Web. He wrote the first web browser and server in 1990, while employed at CERN in Switzerland. He is Director of the World Wide Web Consortium (W3C)

Guido van Rossum

is a Dutch programmer who is best known as the creator of the Python language. He was employed by Google from 2005 to 2012, where he spent half his time developing the Python language. In January 2013, he started working for Dropbox

Linus Torvalds

(Born 1969) is a Finnish-American software engineer who is the creator of the Linux kernel which became the kernel for the Linux operating system, Android, and Chrome OS. He also created the distributed revision control system Git.
